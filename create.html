1. Select Design

Download & open the HTML file of your Smart Card

  • Visit Design Library to see live options.
  • Select the option from the dropdown below.
  • We update our Design Libray once in each month. If you don't like any of our existing one you can wait or Email us your requirements. It will be ready with in 48 Hours.
  • Having trouble? Contact US.


2. Modify .HTML

Open the downloaded HTML file in a text editor.

  • Replace the < !--NAME/DESIGNATION/LINK/..-- > with your name, designation, links ...
  • FYI you can also change number of button / button name.
  • Save your edits.
  • Having trouble? Contact US.


3. Upload

You have 3 options. 1. Upload in your own server, 2. Upload in our GitHub. 3. Send your file, we will upload in our server for you.

  • OPTION 1# Upload in your own server and proceed to next step.
  • OPTION 2# First go to our SMART CARD server. Open a file (Naming convention: mr-dexter). You can check HERE if the name you want is available. And wait up-to 12 hours.
  • OPTION 3# Email us your HTML file. We will do everything else for you. You will get all updates within 12 hours.
  • Having trouble? Contact US.


4. Create QR

Create & Download your QR code.

  • OPTION 1# Paste your smart card URL below & create your QR code.
  • OPTION 2# Your URL is https://onescan.github.io/sc/name_of_your_folder/.
    Create QR code using this URL.
  • Having trouble? Contact US.
