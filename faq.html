FAQ



What is this about?

Right now our application let you create QR code readable business card. We are working on more similar technologies.

Can I make my card by myself?

Yes, our application lets you make one by yourself. However, if you face any difficulties we can do that for you.

What if I don't like the Smart Card / QR design?

We use standard designs which commonly fit most people. In case of custom design, contact us with your design requirements. We will be happy to do that for you!

How much One Scan service costs?

Our service is FREE for everyone!

What if I want to change a link or button on a existing smart-card?

You can do it by yourself. Go inside of your file and edit your changes. Our standard approval time is up to 6 hours. Or you can contact us with your changes.

What are the benifits of a One Scan Smart Card?

1. You are saving this planet by reducing paper waste.
2. Cost efficiency. You don't have to pay anything for creating or making any new changes to your card. Which is quite opposite of paper cards.
3. Being smart! Don't need to pack your wallet with so many cards while you have empty storage on your mobile device.
